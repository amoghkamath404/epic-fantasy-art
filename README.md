## 🧠 Prompt

A valiant knight on a powerful warhorse charges through a volcanic landscape under a red, storm-lashed sky. The knight wears ornate, weathered armor with intricate embossing and riveted plates; he holds a sharp, ornate medieval blade aloft, blade gleaming with runic etchings. Behind them, a volcanic eruption roars, lava spraying and ash clouds glowing in crimson light. The scene is rendered in cinematic painterly detail with rich textures, bold brushwork, and dramatic glow on metal armor. Foreground features cracked ground and jagged rocks; distant smoke-darkened mountains loom. Lighting is dramatic, with red-backlit silhouettes, warm lava tones, and cool steel highlights. Perspective from a low angle to amplify scale. Aspect ratio 16:9, 8K resolution (7680x4320). Style: epic fantasy concept art, painterly.
